About Biomedix Pharma

As a leading medicine importer, Biomedix Pharma is dedicated to sourcing and delivering premium healthcare products from trusted global manufacturers. We ensure that every medicine meets rigorous quality and safety standards for the well-being of our communities.

Our Story

Founded in 2023, Biomedix Pharma emerged with a mission to bridge the gap between international pharmaceutical excellence and local healthcare needs. We recognized the urgent need for safe, high-quality medicines in Bangladesh and committed ourselves to become a trusted importer.

Over the years, our expertise in regulatory compliance, logistics, and quality assurance has enabled us to build robust partnerships with top-tier manufacturers worldwide. Today, we proudly supply premium healthcare products to hospitals, pharmacies, and clinics across the region.

Our ongoing journey is fueled by innovation, integrity, and a steadfast commitment to improving patient care and health outcomes.

Our Mission & Vision
